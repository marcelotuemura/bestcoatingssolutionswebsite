# Project Content Guide

## Required project information (before publish)

- Slug (URL-safe)
- Title and summary (EN + ES)
- Category
- Publication status `published`
- Owner approval status `approved`
- At least one approved image **or** a clear textual case study without implying missing photos are real work

## Optional project information

- Vessel type, manufacturer, model, year
- General location (public region/city only)
- Damage type, repair scope, process, materials, finish notes
- Completion date
- Before/during/after images and before-after pairs
- Related service slugs
- Testimonial reference (only if that testimonial is also published)

## Customer privacy

Never publish:

- HIN numbers
- Insurance claim numbers
- Customer contact details
- Exact private slip locations
- Unapproved vessel-identifying information

## Vessel identification

Prefer generic vessel type language unless the customer consents to naming details.

## HIN prohibition

HIN must never appear in copy, filenames, alt text, captions, or metadata.

## Customer consent

Project photography and identifiable details require documented publication consent before `published`.

## Marina / location privacy

Use general public locations only (for example, South Florida or Fort Lauderdale). Do not publish private marina slip identifiers.

## Before/after photography

- Keep camera height, distance, and lighting as consistent as practical.
- Label placeholders clearly when real photos are not approved.
- See `docs/assets/PORTFOLIO_IMAGE_GUIDE.md`.

## Technical review

Confirm repair narrative accuracy and that no unsupported guarantees appear.

## Publication approval

Both `status: 'published'` and `ownerApprovalStatus: 'approved'` are required for production visibility and sitemap inclusion.
