# Legal review checklist

Website Privacy and Terms pages are **content architecture**, not certified legal advice.

Owner / counsel should decide before production:

- [ ] Governing law and venue
- [ ] Limitation of liability language
- [ ] Warranty disclaimer final wording
- [ ] Data retention periods for contact/estimate/photo data
- [ ] Whether any formal privacy regimes apply (do not claim GDPR/CCPA/etc. without confirmation)
- [ ] Business entity display details (legal name already in `siteConfig`; no invented registration numbers)
- [ ] Whether a physical mailing address must be published
- [ ] Children’s privacy age threshold confirmation
- [ ] Third-party processor list once email/CRM/analytics are chosen
- [ ] Consent record retention for form acknowledgments

Do not invent licenses, certifications, street addresses, or compliance seals.
