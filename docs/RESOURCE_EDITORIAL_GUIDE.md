# Resource Editorial Guide

## Topic selection

Prefer educational topics that help owners prepare for estimates, inspections, and scope conversations.

Safe examples: estimate information, damage photography, gelcoat signs, color-matching inspection needs, written scope understanding.

## Safety boundaries

Do **not** publish hazardous or high-risk DIY instructions, including:

- Grinding fiberglass
- Spraying coatings
- Structural laminate repair steps
- Resin catalyst ratios
- Hazardous chemical handling
- Confined-space coating work
- Structural engineering conclusions

Encourage professional evaluation for uncertain or potentially structural damage.

## Technical review

Have a technical reviewer confirm accuracy and safety boundaries before owner approval.

## Localization

Provide complete EN and ES copy for title, summary, sections, and metadata.

## Metadata

Each published resource needs `metaTitle` and `metaDescription` in both locales.

## Update dates

Maintain `publishedAt` and `updatedAt` when content changes materially.

## Author and reviewer handling

Use **Best Coatings Solutions** as organizational author unless an approved personal byline exists. Do not fabricate credentials.

## Publication status

Only `published` + owner-approved resources appear on the index, in the sitemap, and in Article structured data.
